My Journey in Tech

Published on March 25, 2025

My journey in tech began when I first discovered programming during my college years. I started with C++, which opened my eyes to the power of solving complex problems through code. One of my proudest moments was building the website for Strayniya Welfare Association (SWA), where I combined my skills in web development and database management to create a platform that supports community initiatives. Along the way, I’ve faced challenges like debugging tricky issues and learning new technologies, but each obstacle has made me a better developer. I’m excited to continue this journey, building solutions that make a difference.
