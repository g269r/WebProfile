A Shayari on Creativity and a Question for the World

Published on March 25, 2025

Shayari on Creativity:

Code likhta hoon main dil se, khayalon ke rangon se,
Har line mein chhupi hai ek kahani, jo ban jati hai nayi duniya meri zindagi se.

This shayari reflects how creativity drives my passion for coding. Each line of code I write is like a story, building new worlds through technology.

A Question for the World:

In a world driven by technology, how do we balance innovation with the human connection that makes us who we are? I’d love to hear your thoughts—feel free to share them with me!
